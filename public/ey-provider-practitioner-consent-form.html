Consent to Use Personal Details

In order to complete the Early Years Financial Incentive Service and to comply with data protection and confidentiality regulations, we are required to obtain written consent to use personal details as part of the service.

How we may use your personal details

Your personal details may be used for the Early Years Financial Incentive Service, and be shared with the Department for Education (DfE), to start a claim on your behalf for the 1000 incentive payment available for new and returning early years practitioners.

What personal details will be used

To start a claim on your behalf, we will need your consent to provide the below personal details:

       full name

       email address

       start date
